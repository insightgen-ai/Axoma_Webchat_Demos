About Niterra — Beyond the spark

Niterra is one of the world’s leading technical ceramics and automotive suppliers, serving Original Equipment, Aftermarket, and industrial customers globally. Headquartered in Nagoya, Japan, we have a presence on every continent.

Our product portfolio for the automotive industry includes spark plugs, glow plugs, ignition coils, ignition leads under the NGK Ignition Parts brand, and advanced sensors, EGR valves, throttle bodies under NTK Vehicle Electronics.
